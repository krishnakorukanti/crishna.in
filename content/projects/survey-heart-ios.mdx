# SurveyHeart for iOS
## Technology Stack

SurveyHeart for iOS is built using modern technologies to deliver a seamless user experience:

- **Swift and SwiftUI**: Leveraging the power of Swift and SwiftUI, SurveyHeart delivers a fast, responsive, and visually stunning user interface across all iOS devices.

- **RealmDB**: Utilizing RealmDB for data storage and management, SurveyHeart ensures data integrity and seamless synchronization across devices.

- **MVVM Architecture**: Following the Model-View-ViewModel (MVVM) architecture pattern, SurveyHeart separates concerns, making the codebase modular, testable, and maintainable.

SurveyHeart for iOS provides users with a comprehensive solution for creating, distributing, and analyzing surveys on the go. Whether you're a professional researcher, educator, or business owner, SurveyHeart empowers you to gather valuable insights and make data-driven decisions with ease.
